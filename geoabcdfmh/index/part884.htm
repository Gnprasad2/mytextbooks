Telegraph and Telephone Service


Telegraph services were introduced at Calcutta in 1851 and a telephone service began also at Calcutta in 1851-82 soon after their invention in the United States of America. Today there are over 45,000 telegraph offices. There are over 25,000 telephone exchanges in the country by 2005-06. Ours is the largest telecom network in Asia.
