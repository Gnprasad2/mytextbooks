7. The Tertiary System


The final fragmentation of the Gondwana took place during the Tertiary Period. There occurred faulting of the Peninsula alongwith the subsidence of the broken blocks beneath the Arabian Sea and the Bay of Bengal. The Tertiary rocks are found in Kathiawar, Kachchh (Gujarat), Laki Series (Rajasthan), and along the Coromandal and Malabar coasts. In north-east, they are found in the Meghalaya Plateau; the Jaintia Series.
