Phase III: 1980-90


Following the success of the SLV, ISRO was keen to begin construction of a satellite launch vehicle that would be able to put truly useful satellite into polar orbit. The Augmented Satellite Launch Vehicle (ASLV) was tested in 1987, but this launch was a failure. After minor corrections, another launch was attempted in 1988, and this launch again failed.
