Organisation and its leadership

 Organized groups are more powerful and more efficient than the less organized groups.

 A good leadership can also provide better concerted efforts and better management and channelization of resources, towards success of the group. E.g

Kailash Styarthi as a Nobel Laureate can better advocate and champion the cause of Child rights.

 A good leadership can also provide the organization with:

1. Acute political Skills

2. Contacts

3. Developed media Skills

4. A high public profile.
