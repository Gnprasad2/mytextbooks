Aim:


 To re-define innovations to go beyond formal R&D parameters.

 Facilitate innovative solutions that lead to inclusive growth for the people and by the people.

 Foster an innovation eco-system across domains and sectors to strengthen entrepreneurship.

 Focus on key drivers to ensure sustainability, durability and quality and expand the space for dialogue and discourse on innovation.

 NInC will also be involved in facilitating State level and sectoral innovations. This will create an eco- system to boost innovation performance in the country.
