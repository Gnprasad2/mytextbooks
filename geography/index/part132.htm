Mesosphere

➢This layer extends between 50 km and 80 km. Temperature again starts decreasing with increase in height in this layer. This is because this layer is being heated from below. The ozonosphere present in the stratosphere provides the heat. The upper most limit of mesosphere is 80 km where the temperature becomes - 80°C, and is called as mesopause. This is the height from where the air in the atmosphere starts inducing some frictional resistance on any moving object. When the meteors, which are also known as shooting stars enters the mesosphere, they get heated due tothe frictional resistance experienced by them due to air.
