13.1. Exchanges in India

The stock exchanges are important player of the capital market. They are the platform of trading in securities and as such they assists and control the buying and selling of securities. Thus, stocks exchanges constitute of a market where securities issued1 by the central and state, governments, public bodies and joint stock companies are traded.
