66. ASTRA (BEYOND VISUAL RANGE AIR-TO-AIR MISSILE)

 Astra is a BVR Air-to-Air Missile having high Single Shot Kill Probability (SSKP), and is very reliable.

 It is an all aspect, all weather missile with active Radar terminal guidance.

 It has excellent ECCM features, smokeless propulsion and process improved effectiveness in multi-target scenarios.

 It can be launched in both autonomous and buddy mode operation with features for lock-on before

launch (LOBL) and lock-on after launch (LOAL).
