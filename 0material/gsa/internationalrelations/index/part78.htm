3.2.2. Elections

 In December 2017, Nepal held Parliamentary Elections. The Lower House, House of Representatives (Pratindihi Sabha), comprises of 275 seats, out of which 165 are elected on the FPTP system and 110 through the proportional representation system.

 

 Elections for Upper house, National Assembly (Rashtiriya Sabha,) were held in February 2018, it has 59 members.
