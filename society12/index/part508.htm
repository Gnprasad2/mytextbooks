Benefits

 The implementation of the ICPS has resulted in creditable improvement in the funding and monitoring of childcare institutions (CCIs), as well as in the functioning of statutory bodies like CWCs and JJBs.

 As a programme, the ICPS has aided in the improvement of the existing infrastructure for child protection. Yet, the scheme is still very much in a scale up mode.
