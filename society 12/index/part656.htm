7. The transgender community has been among one of the most marginalized communities in India. Discuss. How does the Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Bill 2016 seek to protect transgenders from discrimination and address the problems faced by them?

Approach:

 Firstly, briefly define what constitutes the transgender community.

 Next, highlight the issues related to the marginalization of transgender community in India.

 Delineate the provisions for healthcare, education, certificate of identity etc in the Bill.

 Briefly highlight the shortcomings of the bill and suggest what further can be done. Supreme Court judgments can also be used for suggestions.
