10. Portrayal of stereotypical sensational images of women not only reduces their identity to a mere object of desire but also reinforces the patriarchal structure of the society. Discuss with examples.

Approach:

 Introduce by giving the current status of women i.e. how they are portrayed.

 With examples provide the true picture of different sectors which portray women as commodities.

 Discuss how it reinforces patriarchy.

 Conclude by providing a solution to this problem.
